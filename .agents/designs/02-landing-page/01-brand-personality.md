# Brand Personality — SkillBridge AI

## Personality Dimensions

| Dimension   | Position         | Rationale |
|-------------|-----------------|-----------|
| Energy      | Calm → **Energetic** (60%) | Students need motivation, but the product is serious |
| Formality   | **Professional** (65%) | Career credentialing must feel trustworthy |
| Density     | **Spacious** | Marketing page — every element needs room to breathe |
| Mood        | **Confident** | "You can close these gaps. Here's how." |
| Aesthetic   | **Rich** | Multiple accents, gradient, glassmorphism — high quality feel |

## Visual Style Archetype: 🌅 Cosmic Campus / Hybrid

**Why Cosmic Campus?**
- Students love the premium feel of dark-mode apps (Linear, Notion) but need warmth and approachability
- Indigo = intelligence, technology, trust (brand pillar: AI-powered analysis)
- Cyan = data, market intelligence, information flow
- Emerald = growth, career progress, completion (CTA color)
- Dark hero/footer frames the bright content sections → "premium edtech" feel, not generic SaaS

**Reference products for mood**:
- **Duolingo**: Gamified feel, student-friendly, progress-focused
- **Linear**: Premium dark aesthetic for serious tools
- **Coursera**: Credentialing, professional outcomes

## Color Rationale

| Role | Color | Why |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary | Indigo `#6366F1` | Intelligence, AI, trust |
| Secondary | Cyan `#22D3EE` | Data, market insight |
| CTA | Emerald `#10B981` | Growth, success, action |
| Dark BG | `#0B0E14` | Obsidian — premium, not black |
| Light BG | `#FAFAFA` | Near-white, easy on eyes |

## Typography Rationale

- **Nunito**: Rounded, friendly, student-facing. Bold weights (800–900) for impactful headlines. Used by Duolingo. Approachable but not childish.
- **Inter**: Neutral, readable body text. Industry standard for SaaS.
- **JetBrains Mono**: Stats, numbers, data points. Signals "this is real data", not marketing fluff.

## Motion Rationale

- **Blob float (8s)**: Background life without distraction
- **Card hover lift (translateY -6px)**: Signals interactivity
- **Button glow pulse**: CTA draws the eye
- **Scroll-triggered fade-in**: Sections reveal as user scrolls — professional, not bouncy
- **Header blur on scroll**: Glass morphism when content slides under
